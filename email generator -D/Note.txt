### Description
The Email Generator is a user-friendly web application that creates unique 
email addresses based on customizable parameters. It allows users to specify 
the length and character types for the email username, choose from popular 
email services, and select a domain extension. The app provides a quick and 
easy way to generate random email addresses for various purposes, such as 
creating temporary accounts or brainstorming username ideas. With its 
intuitive interface and instant results, the Email Generator streamlines 
the process of coming up with new email addresses.

### Features
1. Customizable email length (5-20 characters)
2. Character type selection (lowercase, uppercase, numbers, symbols)
3. Choice of popular email services (Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, Outlook)
4. Selection of domain extensions (.com, .net, .org, .io)
5. One-click email generation
6. Copy to clipboard functionality
7. Responsive design for mobile and desktop use
8. Real-time email preview
9. Input validation to ensure at least one character type is selected
10. Clear and user-friendly interface
